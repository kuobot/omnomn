Why I Joined a Startup

Sep 16th, 2012

I vividly remember the day I met the founder of my startup. I was like the little boy in Polar Express. The train had just stopped outside my window on Christmas Eve. “Welcome aboard!” he yelled. I didn’t care where we were going. I was simply estatic about being onboard. This was my chance at leaping headfirst into the startup world. It was now or never.

I lept. Little did I know it would be the ride of my life.
