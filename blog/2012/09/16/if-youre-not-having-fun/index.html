If You're Not Having Fun, You're Probably Not Doing It Right

Sep 16th, 2012

I overheard our head of sales say this out loud just last Friday.

I forget this sometimes. But it’s probably because I get lost in the trenches battling fires all day long. But how does one become Top Gun? The answer is the one who has all the fun is the one who makes up the rules of the game which everyone else plays by. It was getting tiring and I was exhausted at the end of every day. It was out of desperation that I picked up enough of Rails to be able to play. To sculpt. Little by little, I regained my strength.

Today I set goals. I shared them with others who I considered my strongest allies who were most likely to help in my endeavors. I made headway. Or at least, I felt as though I had. Which felt pretty darn good.

And I bought an electric guitar to help maintain my sanity.

Great balls of fire.
