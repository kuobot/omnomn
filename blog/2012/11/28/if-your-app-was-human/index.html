If Your App Was Human

Nov 28th, 2012

Javascript(?) giving your app personality.

First it was about features. Feature war Now it’s about removing features Forget about inventing new experiences. What about cleaning up the existing ones we have to suffer though in our daily lives?

Omnomn helping app. Just built a prototype and the hyperlink made the feeling rather snappy. It was a bit harsh. I wanted to make it smooth and sexy. Or perhaps bouncy and a bit playful. This is the genius of the likes of Steve Jobs and Walt Disney. I felt like I was standing on the shoulders of great men.
