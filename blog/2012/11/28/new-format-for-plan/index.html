New Format for Plan

Nov 28th, 2012

App Connections page should look like: https://www.easel.io/documents/e3801d92405f7955#e3801d92405f7955 Finished Campaign analytics should look like: https://www.easel.io/documents/88b0ddec331fb7c1?#88b0ddec331fb7c1

Issue #1: AM is confused when different stats appear in admin blast data vs. in app Issue #2: Customers may be confused by numbers that are displayed w/o an explanation as to what they mean (eg. 2 customer counts). Issue #3: Customers want to see campaign result detail (I thought I had this many customers, why does it say only this many got reached?) Issue #4: Terminology is all over the place. Eg. it’s “Runs from” and “runs to” in one place but “Start” and “end” in another. Issue #5: Still outstanding is to decide how to treat single email/multiple entries. Issue #6: FB fan inclusion into stats Issue #7: Terms need to be defined.

Resolution #1: AM should seldom have to go into admin. Resolution #2: We make it clear which numbers mean what and add tooltips where necessary Resolution #3: Reach is defined as FB+emails+text+bounce Resolution #4: Lets go w/ Start and end when referring to campaigns. Resolution #5: Waiting on Sailesh. Resolution #6: Include Fb fans into stats Resolution #7: I defined some terms.

Quick fix changes:

  1. In admin, replace the words “Aggregate Information” with “Stats” and change that data in admin to reflect same exact data as what is shown to customers Emails: 6089.0; Opens: 940.0; Visits: 285.0; Optouts: 23.0; Bounces: 4.0
  2. In admin, make “Experiment” the same size as the other 2 headers.
  3. In admin, hide Experiments if we are not actively using.
  4. In admin, replace the words “Blast Groups” with “Sent to Mailgun”
