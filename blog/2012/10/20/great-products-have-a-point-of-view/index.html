Great Products Have a Point of View

Oct 20th, 2012

Saumil reminded me of this again. To be in front of the hockey puck means sticking yourself out there. You tell the world what you’re about and rally up those folks who believe in your vision. Early adopters willing to eat the dogfood you make alongside you, investors willing to back nothing but a dream and a team.

In fact, if you don’t have a unique perspective on things, then you’re an execution game. Doing something which is obvious, but doing it better. But if you think of the entire world as operating on that local maximum graph, you’re on that same little hump. Starting a brand new one is far riskier and you’d be venturing into unknown territory. This is where blue oceans are created.
