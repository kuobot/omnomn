Whimsy

Oct 20th, 2012

What is the New Web about?

Whimsy, personality, fun. Character.

This is why copyrighting is so important these days.

Gone: Heavy spec writing. Instead: Lightweight, standups instead.

Gone: The unknown corporation. Instead: The personable touch.

Gone: Ignoring the customer Instead: The customer is king.

Gone: Poor user experiences Instead: Spotlight on the user experience

Gone: Just build it Instead: Figure out what you will build and market and sell it first. Then build it

Gone: Silos of demarcatation Instead: Integrated, small design-build teams who can turn on a dime.

Gone: Boring jobs Instead: You can have your cake and eat it too

Gone: The corporate zombie Instead: The enlightened, self-motivated employee

Gone: Playing by the rules Intead: Making the rules. Or in some cases, breaking them first

Gone: Believing you can’t. Instead: Believing you can.

Gone: Traditional models and institutions Instead: Forging your own way

Gone: Traditional glass ceilings Instead: Fuck you money
