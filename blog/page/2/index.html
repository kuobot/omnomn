Build and Build Fast

Nov 1st, 2012

Time to procure 3 icons: 3 minutes. Previous time to procurement: at least 20 minutes. Here’s the breakdown:

I’d have to remember where I’d put those vector icon files. Then it would take a few mins to fire up an Adobe product. I think my MacBook Pro was resisting Photoshop. I had several sets, and not knowing which one had the ones I needed, I’d open all of them. Wait a few more minutes. Then I’d have to sift through all of them. It was like finding a needle in a haystack. And this was me having icon sets already. Imagine if one didn’t have them. Tack on another half hour minimum spent online sifting through all the icon sets on the internet, trying to find the best bang for your buck. Makes my hair stand on end just thinking about it.
