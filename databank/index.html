What's the Databank?

The Databank is where we gather amazing talent from around the web.

If you think you have what it takes to be a part of The Databank, gather:

• Your name.
• Your most amazing stuff.
• A brief description of your ideal work.


and send it on over to us! We’ll see if there’s something we think might be of interest to you in the world of omnomn. In the event there isn’t anything, we’ll store your information and will let you know as opportunities arise.


That’s it! Have fun, go forth and conquer. :)
